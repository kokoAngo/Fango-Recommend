flowchart TD
  A([Start]) --> B[Upload: PDF + Doc]
  B --> C[Parse & Store Inputs]
  C --> D[Randomly Sample 10 Sets]
  D --> F[Display 10 Sets to User]


  F --> G[Loop Counter = 1]

  G --> H[User Selects / Accepts Results]
  H --> I[Record Feedback (Accepted/Rejected + Optional Notes)]
  I --> J[Analyze Feedback to Update User Profile]

  J --> K[Generate Recommendations Based on Updated Profile]
  K --> L[Show Recommendations to User]


  L --> M[Loop Counter = Loop Counter + 1]

  M --> N{Loop Counter <= 3?}


  N -- Yes --> Go back to G
  N -- No --> Z([End])
